## Create a new cadence

### Create a new cadence from scratch

1. Navigate to the cadence page.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-management-001.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>

2. After that, click on the "Build Cadence" button.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-management-002.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>

3. There are several ways to create a cadence:

- **Start from scratch**: This option allows you to create a new cadence from scratch.
- **AI assisted**:
- **Pre-formatted**:

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-management-003.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>

4. After clicking "From scratch", "New Cadence" page will be shown.
   You should add information about cadence name and owner.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-management-004.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>

5. After that, you will be redirected to the cadence builder page. You should add cadence steps to created cadence newly.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-management-005.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>
