Explore our flexible upgrade plans designed to suit your needs, whether you're an individual user or part of a larger organization. Each plan offers a variety of features and benefits that enhance your experience, providing you with the tools necessary to maximize productivity and efficiency.

### Free Plan

- Cost: $0
- Features:
  - 10 Export Credits/Month: Perfect for light users who need basic functionality.
  - 5 Mobile Number Credits/Year: Stay connected with limited mobile number access.

### Basic Plan

- Cost: $59 per user, per month (billed monthly)
- Features:
  - 1,000 Export Credits/Month: Ideal for users who require more frequent exports.
  - 75 Mobile Number Credits/Month: Increased mobile connectivity for enhanced communication.

### Professional Plan

- Cost: $99 per user, per month (billed monthly)
- Features:
  - 2,000 Export Credits/Year: Suitable for professionals needing substantial export capabilities.
  - 100 Mobile Number Credits/Year: Ample mobile number access for business needs.

- Cost: $149 per user, per month (billed monthly)
