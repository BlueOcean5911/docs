title: Outbound Call
---

Outbound calls are an essential part of our communication process. This guide will help you navigate the system to make calls efficiently and log them accurately. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth calling experience.

1. **Navigate to the Calls Page**  
   Start by going to the **Calls** page in your application. Here, you will find the list of available phone numbers, including the **AIVIO** phone number, which you will use for outbound calls.

2. **Select the AIVIO Phone Number**  
   Once you're on the Calls page, locate and select the **AIVIO** phone number. This selection is crucial as it enables you to make calls using the designated number for your outbound communications.

3. **Initiate a Manual Call**  
   After selecting the number, look for the phone icon at the bottom left of the screen. Clicking this icon will allow you to initiate a manual call. Ensure that your device is connected and ready to make calls.
