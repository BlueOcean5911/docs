## Manage cadence steps

In the cadence builder page, you can manage a cadence by clicking on the "Edit" button.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-steps-management-001.png"
  alt="Cadence Step Management Light"
/>

You can edit and move up or move down and delete the cadence step in the cadence builder page.
