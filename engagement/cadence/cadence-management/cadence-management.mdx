## Create a new cadence

### Create a new cadence from scratch

1. **Navigate to the Cadence Page**  
    Start by going to the cadence management page where you can view existing cadences and create new ones.

   <img
     className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
     src="/images/cadence-management-001.png"
     alt="Cadence Management Light"
   />

2. **Click on the "Build Cadence" Button**  
   Look for the prominent "Build Cadence" button on the page and click it to begin the process of creating a new cadence.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-management-002.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>

3. **Choose a Creation Method**  
   There are several ways to create a cadence:
   - **Start from Scratch**: This option allows you to create a new cadence from the ground up, customizing every detail.
   - **AI Assisted**: Utilize AI to help generate a cadence based on your preferences and requirements.
   - **Pre-Formatted**: Select from pre-existing templates that can be modified to suit your needs.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-management-003.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>

4. **Fill Out the "New Cadence" Form**  
   After selecting "From Scratch," you will be directed to the "New Cadence" page. Here, fill in the required information, including the cadence name and the owner.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-management-004.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>

5. **Access the Cadence Builder Page**  
    Once you have submitted the form, you will be redirected to the cadence builder page. This is where you can add steps to your newly created cadence. Define each step clearly to ensure a smooth workflow.

   <img
     className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
     src="/images/cadence-management-005.png"
     alt="Cadence Management Light"
   />

## Update Cadence

### Update Cadence Name and Owner

To update the cadence name and owner, navigate to the "Settings" tab located in the top right corner of the individual cadence page. Make your changes and save them to update the cadence information.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-setting-001.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>

### Add Star to Cadence

To mark a cadence as important or favorite, you can add a star to it. This feature allows for quick access to frequently used cadences.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-star-001.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>

### Make a Cadence Inactive or Active

You have the option to toggle the status of a cadence between active and inactive. This is useful for managing cadences that are currently not in use.

<img
  className="block rounded-2xl shadow-lg"
  src="/images/cadence-active-001.png"
  alt="Cadence Management Light"
/>

## Delete Cadence

If you need to remove a cadence permanently, you can do so by following the deletion process. Be cautious, as this action cannot be undone.
